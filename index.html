// SYSTEM NOTES

This design emerged from watching systems fail in real-time—governments cracking down, agencies collapsing under their own weight, constitutional guardrails being tested. The CRT terminal aesthetic felt right: the monochrome glow of a monitoring station, alerts trickling in at different urgency levels. I wanted each story to feel like a data packet in a queue, waiting to be acknowledged. The phosphor green is both nostalgic and slightly ominous—like watching a control room in a film from the 1980s where something is about to go very wrong.
